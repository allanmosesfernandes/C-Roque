Before you arrive

Information for guests

You've probably noticed that the hotel is exclusive and right on the beach, providing you with unparalleled privacy and peace.

Road access is limited and for your comfort, we have a jeep that will pick you up on your day of arrival and drop you off on your day of departure. Any extra transfers will be charged up to INR 100 per transfer.

Jeep transfers are available from 8 am to 11 pm, trips are priced at INR 100 per way. FREE TRIPS ARE AT 9:00HRS, 13:00HRS, 18:00HRS.

Children below 6 yrs of age are free when sharing the bed with parents.

Above 6 yrs of age the child will compulsorily need an extra bed and would be charged the rate of an adult.

Extra adult rate Rs. 800 per night. This is inclusive of an extra mattress and the Self Service Breakfast.

We take an advance payment of 1 night stay 14 days prior to your arrival date.

If a guest is staying for just one night the full amount will be taken as advance payment.

The advance payment is non refundable.

In case your stay is more than a week we will need 2 night advance payment.

For guests staying less than 7 nights, the balance amount of your room charges to be paid on arrival at the hotel.

For guests staying more than 7 nights, the balance payment can be made in parts during your stay with us.

Reservation must be cancelled 14 days prior to the planned date of arrival.

Reservations cancelled within 7-14 days of arrival date will incur a cancellation fee equivalent to one night's accommodation charge.

Reservations cancelled 7 days after the arrival date will have no refund.

Reservations cannot be cancelled or amended once you arrive at the hotel.
